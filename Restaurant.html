THE BEST RESTAURANT IN ISTANBUL: Byzantion Bistro Restaurant

Byzantion Bistro Restaurant introduces customers to the taste of Ottoman palace cuisine with years of experiences.
They serve the unique tastes that were presented between the 14th and 18th centuries, which had almost forgotten.
They are ready to welcome you with a cuisine where fruits are used instead of tomatoes, corn, potatoes to flavor all foods.
They also serve the most popular Turkish and international dishes in the world. Pomegranate flower sorbet.
Stuffed melon, sour cherry stuffed grape leaves and levzine are just a few options from Ottoman Palace cuisine.
Byzantion Bistro restaurant, Turkish and Ottoman cuisine, is located near Aksaray tram station, with capacity.
120 people inside and 30 people per side of the garden.
